# Design Tokens

Tokens are the smallest building blocks of the Mavka Design System. They store visual design attributes such as color, typography, and spacing.

## Colors

Mavka uses a curated palette inspired by nature, with Emerald as our primary brand color.

## Typography

We use **Inter** as our primary typeface for clarity and readability across all screens.

| Level | Size | Weight | usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Display | 48px | Bold | Hero sections |
| Heading 1 | 32px | Semibold | Page titles |
| Heading 2 | 24px | Semibold | Section headers |
| Body | 16px | Regular | Main content |

## Spacing

Our spacing system is based on a 4px grid.

- **sm**: 4px
- **md**: 8px
- **lg**: 16px
- **xl**: 24px
- **2xl**: 32px

## Elevations

Elevations are used to create depth and hierarchy.

- **Flat**: No shadow.
- **Raised**: Subtle shadow for cards and buttons.
- **Overlay**: Deep shadow for modals and tooltips.
