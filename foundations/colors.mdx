# Colors

Mavka's color system is designed to be harmonious, vibrant, and accessible. We use a combination of primary brand colors and a comprehensive neutral scale.

## Brand Colors

Our brand colors are inspired by the organic beauty of nature.

## Neutral Scale

Used for text, backgrounds, and borders.

| Level | Hex | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, surface containers |
| Gray 50 | `#F9FAFB` | Subtle backgrounds |
| Gray 200 | `#E5E7EB` | Borders and dividers |
| Gray 500 | `#6B7280` | Secondary text |
| Gray 900 | `#111827` | Primary text and headings |
