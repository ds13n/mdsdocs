# Typography

Typography plays a critical role in the Mavka Design System, ensuring clarity, hierarchy, and a premium reading experience.

## Font Family

We use **Inter** as our primary typeface. It is chosen for its exceptional readability on screens and its modern, professional aesthetic.

```css
font-family: 'Inter', system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif;
```

## Scale

Our typographic scale follows a modular hierarchy to ensure consistent vertical rhythm.

| Token | Size | Line Height | Weight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Display** | 48px | 1.1 | 700 (Bold) |
| **H1** | 32px | 1.25 | 600 (Semibold) |
| **H2** | 24px | 1.3 | 600 (Semibold) |
| **H3** | 20px | 1.4 | 500 (Medium) |
| **Body** | 16px | 1.6 | 400 (Regular) |
| **Caption** | 12px | 1.5 | 400 (Regular) |

## Usage Guidelines

- **Headings**: Use `600` or `700` weight for clear hierarchy.
- **Body Text**: Maintain a line-height of at least `1.5` for optimal readability.
- **Letter Spacing**: Use `-0.01em` or `-0.02em` for headings to give them a premium, tight look.
