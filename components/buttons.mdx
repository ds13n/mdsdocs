# Buttons

Buttons allow users to take actions and make choices with a single tap.

## Variants

Mavka provides three main button variants to establish clear action hierarchy.

### Primary Button

Used for the most important action on a page.

```tsx
<Button variant="primary">Click Me</Button>
```

- **Background**: Emerald 500
- **Text**: White
- **Corner Radius**: 8px

### Secondary Button

Used for secondary actions that support the primary goal.

```tsx
<Button variant="secondary">Learn More</Button>
```

- **Background**: Gray 100
- **Text**: Gray 900
- **Border**: Gray 200

### Ghost Button

Used for low-emphasis actions or within tight spaces.

```tsx
<Button variant="ghost">Cancel</Button>
```

- **Background**: Transparent
- **Text**: Emerald 600

## Interactive States

Buttons include subtle micro-animations for interactive feedback:

- **Hover**: Scale up by 2% and subtly brighten the background.
- **Active (Pressed)**: Scale down by 2% and darken the background.
- **Disabled**: Desaturate and reduce opacity to 50%.
