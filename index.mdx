## Premium by Default

Every pixel in Mavka is designed to feel premium. We prioritize subtle animations, precise spacing, and accessible color scales to ensure your users have the best possible experience.

<Tip>
  Mavka uses **Inter** for typography and **Emerald** as its primary interaction color. Every component adheres to a strict 4px grid.
</Tip>
